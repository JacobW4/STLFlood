About Us

We are students at the University of Health Sciences and Pharmacy in Saint Louis working together to provide a solution to flooding in University City. We have created an incident reporting system utilizing resources from IBM. This system is being used to record flooding incidents so that we can predict where the next flood will occur in University City.
